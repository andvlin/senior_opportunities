Doors of Change

DoorsofChange@gmail.com
9400 Gilman Avenue, La Jolla 92122, CA

Doors of Change is a nonprofit organization that services and supports homeless youths ages 12-24. We offer a safe and supportive environment to come and create art through our weekly Thursday Taking Art to the Streets (TATS) program. TATS facilitates healing and empowerment through the creation of art. www.doorsofchange.org.
Volunteers are needed to help set up and assist with our Art lessons from 3:00 pm to 5:00 pm at the Episcopal Church in Ocean Beach and then assist with dinner from 5:00 pm to 6:00 pm. Interaction with the youth is encouraged.
Ideally we are looking for volunteers that can commit to one Thursday each month. Please contact Shawn Cross the Program Manager.

San Diego House Rabbit Society

SDHRabbitSociety@gmail.com
9400 Gilman Avenue, La Jolla 92122, CA

Volunteer for Escondido Humane Society with adoptable rabbits in the PETCO adoption program. Volunteering with the rabbits includes cleaning two rabbit cages, changing litterboxes, feeding, exercising and socializing up to three rabbits and talk to the people shopping at Petco about rabbit care. Training and educational materials are provided. Requires approx. 2 hours per day and a long term commitment of one or more days per week. You can go any time the store is open. Five locations available: Poway, Carmel Mtn Ranch, Escondido, San Marcos and Mission Gorge (Friars Rd).
